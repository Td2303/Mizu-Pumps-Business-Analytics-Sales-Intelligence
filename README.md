# Mizu Pumps Business Analytics & Sales Intelligence

## 📌 Overview

This repository showcases business analytics workflows developed for a B2C pump distribution business, focusing on sales intelligence, dealer performance, inventory optimization, customer segmentation, and pricing analytics.

The project combines SQL, Power BI, and business intelligence techniques to support data-driven decision-making across procurement, sales, and distribution operations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor revenue and profitability trends.
- Improve inventory turnover and reduce stock-outs.
- Benchmark dealer productivity and sales performance.
- Segment customers based on purchasing behavior.
- Support pricing and procurement decisions using data analytics.

---

## 📊 Key Analysis

### Revenue & Growth Analytics
- Monthly revenue dashboards
- MoM growth tracking using SQL window functions
- Average order value analysis
- Repeat purchase behavior

### Dealer Performance
- Revenue contribution by dealer
- Dealer ranking using DENSE_RANK()
- Productivity and sales benchmarking
- Regional performance analysis

### Inventory Intelligence
- Inventory turnover tracking
- Stock-out analysis
- Procurement optimization
- SKU-level demand insights

### Customer Analytics
- RFM-style customer segmentation
- High-value customer identification
- Purchase frequency analysis
- Customer lifetime value indicators

### Product Profitability
- Gross margin analysis
- Category-wise profitability benchmarking
- Pricing optimization opportunities
- Revenue contribution by SKU

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
